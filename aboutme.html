Hello, my name is Mariana Cantillo and I’m a character animator.

I studied Digital Design and Animation in Anahuac Mayab University in Mérida, Yucatán, México, the city I grew up in. There I explored all the facets of the filmmaking pipeline, either live action or animated from writing scripts to storyboarding, from concept art to rendering, as well as basics of programming to create videogames. That led me to experiment with software such as 3ds Max, Adobe Premiere, After Effects, Photoshop and Maya.

From there I wanted to dig deeper in the amazing art of animation so I enrolled into the Animation Mentor Character Animation Course, where I’m currently taking the Advanced Acting class.
