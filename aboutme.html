Hello, my name is Mariana and I'm a 3D Artist.

I studied Digital Design and Animation at Anahuac Mayab University. There, I explored all the facets of the filmmaking pipeline, either live action or animated. From writing scripts to storyboarding, from concept art to rendering, as well as the basics of programming to create videogames. In order to accomplish school projects, I learn to use software such as 3ds Max, Adobe Premiere, After Effects, Photoshop, Unity and Unreal Engine.

From there I wanted to dig deeper into the amazing art of animation so I enrolled into Animation Mentor's Character Animation Course, which led me to gain experience with Autodesk Maya, in addition to learning more about the animation process and the nuances of the craft.

I'm currently working at FQuattro, an archviz studio, where I've developed my modeling skills even further, as well as getting a better understanding of lighting, rendering and composition.

Ultimately, I want to continue taking different opportunities in the 3D industry, keep learning and chasing new experiences. And hopefully one day working in a Star Wars thing. Who knows, right?
